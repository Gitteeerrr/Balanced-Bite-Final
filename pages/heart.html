The "Power of 10" Innovation

Many people think you need hours at the gym to help your heart, but science shows the Power of 10. Just 10 minutes of brisk walking three times a day can lower blood pressure as effectively as a 30-minute workout. Innovation isn't always about high-tech machines; sometimes it's about making health fit into a busy student schedule.

Heart Care by Age

🏃 Kids/Teens

Strengthen the heart muscle early through active play and sports.

⚖️ Adults

Focus on managing stress and monitoring cholesterol levels.

🚶 Seniors

Keep the heart flexible with low-impact movement like walking or swimming.
