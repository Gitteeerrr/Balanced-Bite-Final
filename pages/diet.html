The Secret of Mindful Eating

Innovation in health isn't just about new superfoods; it's about how we eat. Mindful eating is the practice of being fully present during a meal. Research shows that turning off the TV and putting away your phone while eating helps your brain recognize when you are full, preventing overeating by up to 20%.

"Treat your meal like a meeting with yourself."

Daily Nutrition Tips

1. Water First: Drink a glass before every meal.
2. Fiber Focus: Aim for 3 different colors of veg a day.
3. Healthy Fats: Include avocados or nuts for brain health.
