How your plate protects your pulse.

The Nutrition-Heart Connection

Your heart is a pump, and the food you eat acts as the "fuel" and "maintenance" for that pump. Innovation in heart health has shown that Functional Foods (foods that have a positive effect on health beyond basic nutrition) can actually clean your arteries. For example, fiber acts like a "broom," sweeping away bad cholesterol before it can clog your heart's pipes.

🧂 The Salt Factor

Too much salt (sodium) makes your body hold onto extra water. This extra water goes into your blood, increasing the pressure against your heart. Over time, this "high pressure" can damage your blood vessels.

Tip: Use herbs and spices instead of salt!

🥑 The "Good" Fat Secret

Not all fat is bad! Your heart loves Unsaturated Fats found in avocados, nuts, and fish. These "good fats" help keep your heart rhythm steady and reduce inflammation in your arteries.

Tip: Swap butter for olive oil!

Heart Care by Age Group

👦 Kids/Teens

Avoid high-sugar snacks to keep arteries flexible and healthy for the future.

🧑 Adults

Focus on "Leafy Greens" (Spinach/Kale) which are high in Vitamin K for heart protection.

👵 Seniors

Prioritize Potassium (Bananas/Potatoes) to help the heart maintain a steady beat.
